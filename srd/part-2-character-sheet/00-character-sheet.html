00 Character Sheet

Your Character Sheet consists of three pages. The first two pages are used primarily while playing the game and the last page helps in build and improve your character between sessions of play.

Here in Part 2, we will go over each page and section of the Character Sheet to explain when, where, and how it will all be used. In reviewing the Character Sheet we will also exploring the remaining rules of the game that relate to each section of the Character Sheet.

If you wish to follow along as you read, you can print the Character Sheet found in the appendix and refer to it with each chapter (though each chapter will also include an image of the section to which it is referring.)

Below is an overview each page of the Character Sheet:

Page 1 - Overview
  • Lists your generic capabilities. The page has references for things like how far you can walk, or how good you are at each of the Skills. This page is also where you will track your inventory and your current status, like your Health or if you have taken on a Status Condition.
Page 2 - Abilities
  • Contains the unique Abilities that your character can use. These Abilities include special maneuvers, Spell Abilities, and weapon attacks. The Character Sheet/abilities page of your Character Sheet is used most often during Encounters with hostile enemies.
Page 3 - Builder
  • Guides in creating and Leveling Up your character. This page contains helpful tables used to track what improvements your character gets at each level. This page also has a place to note each Ability you chose for your character.
