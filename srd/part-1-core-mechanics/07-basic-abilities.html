07 Basic Abilities

Trancing

In the world of Kleptonomica, there are two overlapping layers of reality: the #Material_Layer and the #Ethereal_Layer.

The #Material_Layer is the tangible world in which everyday, physical things exist.

The #Ethereal_Layer is the finer, spiritual world containing the #Source and essence of all things. Almost everything on the #Material_Layer has a ghostly replica on the #Ethereal_Layer. Many things, however, only exist on the #Ethereal_Layer with no #Material_Layer presence, like ghosts, #invisible creatures, #magic_auras, and #magic_afterimages.

In the Kleptonomica World, everyone can learn to see a limited distance into the #Ethereal_Layer by transitioning to their third eye. Doing so reveals the #Ethereal version of the #Material things around you, along with new things only visible to the #Ethereal_Eye.

By spending a Beat, you can enter into a #trance. The #trance can be maintained indefinitely. The #trance ends if you fall Unconscious or choose to end it by spending another Beat. While in a #trance, others will know you are #trancing, as they will see some telltale signs, like your glowing eyes, a glowing third eye, or simply your closed eyes and a concentrating look about you.

While #trancing, you can still see most things from the #Material_Layer, but they will seem illuminated, and your total vision will be limited to your #Ether_Vision range (found in your References.) While in a #trance, your #Ethereal_Vision will reveal the things that only exist on the #Ethereal_Layer. For example, you will see ghosts, or enemies that have cast the Invisibility Spell. While in a #trance, your hearing is unaffected, and you can easily follow #noisy or #loud sounds (though a sound may lead you to an entity's last location, not the entity itself.) Your #trance will reveal #magic_afterimages that indicate recently-cast spells. You can also see the #magic_auras of active magical effects. Some magic may also leave spectral objects on the #Ethereal_Layer. These objects can be interacted with while #trancing. For example, the Glyphic Basin leaves a simmering basin of water on the #Ethereal_Layer, upon the surface of which you can trace a passcode that can undo a magic lock.

If you are ever #blinded by non-magical means (including by a Dark Zone), you can use your #Trance to help you see a bit of the #Material_World. While in a #Trance, you will see the #Ethereal duplicate of the #Material_World illuminated with ghostly light. Unfortunately, your vision will also be limited to your #Ether_Vision range.

It's worth noting that the Trait Feature known as Ethersight allows you to maintain your full sight of the #Material_Layer while in a #Trance. This change effectively removes the main downside of #Trancing (leading most entities with the Ethersight Ability to stay in the #Trance at all times.) This change also allows creatures to have limited night vision and protection from being non-magically #blinded, as the #Material_Plane will still appear as its illuminated duplicate, up to their #Ether_Vision range.
