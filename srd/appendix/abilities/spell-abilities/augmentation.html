Mage Score:

  • [..6] No effect.
  • [7..] Choose an Augmentation from the list below.

Each Augmentation cannot be stacked with itself, but can be combined with other Augmentations if this Ability is cast again:

  • Appearance.
    • Alter your height, weight, facial features, the sound of your voice, hair length, coloration, and distinguishing characteristics, if any. You can make yourself appear as a member of another race, though none of your statistics change. You also can't appear as a creature of a different size than you, and your basic shape and limb arrangement stays the same.
  • Defenses.
    • Grow scales, a shell, or some other natural defense. Set your Shield to 2. This Ability ends that Shield is replaced or reaches 0.
  • Movement. Choose one:
    • Glide. You can Glide a Spitting Distance.
    • Jump. You can Jump a Spitting Distance.
    • Swimming. You can Swim a Spitting Distance. You can also breathe under water.
  • Offenses.
    • Grow claws, fangs, spines, horns, or some other natural weapon. If used instead of any other melee weapon, your natural weapons add 1 to the Damage of your Melee Attack and can change its Damage Type to Solid/Sharp Damage, (if it makes sense to.)

Upgrades:

  • Animalistic. Requires Level 10.
    • Gain the following Augmentation options:
      • Night Vision.
        • If a non-magical Dark Zone would give you Disadvantage, you can ignore that Disadvantage.
      • Wings.
        • You can Fly a Spitting Distance.
  • Lasting.
    • This Ability no longer requires Concentration for just one effect to remain active. (Every effect after the first still requires Concentration.)
  • Multiplicative. Requires Level 5.
    • Gain the following Augmentation options:
      • Arms.
        • Gain one or two more arms.
        • You can Climb a Spitting Distance.
        • You can Wield an additional Item each Round.
      • Legs.
        • Gain one or two more legs.
        • Gain a Resistance to being Slowed.
        • When you use the Dash Ability, you have an Advantage on its Tests.
  • Powerful.
    • Your Defenses Augmentation can provide 1 more Shield and your Offenses Augmentation can provide 1 more Damage.
  • Transmittable.
    • This Ability can now target any creature in Swinging Distance. If the creature is unwilling to be altered, they can resist if they Pass a Grit Save.
