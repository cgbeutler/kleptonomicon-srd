Constitution List

Non-mortal

You have 2 Vitality. When your Vitality reaches 0, you are Dead.

You start with 2 additional Trait Points and 2 additional Knowledge Skill Points.

Once you are Dead, you may use your Reaction to aid an ally if they are within 100 m of where you died by granting one of the following benefits:

  • Restore 1 Vitality to an ally.

Your Trait options are extended to include:
Ethervision : You can double the distance of Sensing.
 Non-Mortal Daily : Select from a list of daily abilities.

Abomination

You are stitched together and given an artificial soul.
When your health reaches 0, you split into 3 chunks out to 2m radius. Your core chunk can only move 1 m and when it takes damage, you lose 1 vitality. Your other pieces can move 3m, grab someone and restrict movement or impose disadvantage. Your core can cast spells that don't require somatic components.

Lich

You have a phylactery and choose the flavor of how it works. You cannot fully die till it is destroyed.

Vampire

You have vampirism. Your power, as a vampire, depends on the traits purchased. All vampires use some shadow magic to protect them from the traditional vampire weaknesses. That magic fades as you do.

Your vitality can only be decreased by fire, wood stakes, and radiant/light. Sunlight also removes 1 vitality per round if your hp is at 0. If you bite someone, you regain 1 vitality.

Living Swarm

You have swarmism and are a living swarm that has taken a humanoid form. Your power, as a living swarm, depends on the traits purchased and the swarm flavor you choose when taking this Constitution.

Your Max Health is halved. Your vitality starts at this new Max Health floor-divided by 10.

When you take this Constitution, you will choose one of the following flavors:

  • Flying Swarm
  • Crawling Swarm
  • Planar Swarm

You then gain the following ability:

Swarmic Evasion
Time: Reaction
When you take Vitality damage, you can use your reaction to do one of the following, depending on your swarm type:

  • Flying Swarm : Use your reaction to move 1 m in any direction, appearing in your Humanoid Form at the destination.
  • Crawling Swarm : Use your reaction to force 1 foe within touch range of you to use their reaction to move their remaining movement away from you. Does not affect creatures immune to the Fear condition.
  • Planar Swarm : This reaction cannot be used if the incoming damage is Ethereal or Silvered. Use your reaction to attempt to ignore the incoming damage. Roll a die and call even or odd. If you guess it correctly, the damage is ignored, otherwise you will take the damage.

This reaction can be used while you are in your Swarm Form as a result of the Swarmify ability (see the Swarmify Trait.)

Swarmify
Time: Flourish
You use your Flourish to dissolve from your humanoid form into a swarm, provoking Opportunity before the rest of this ability takes effect.

You then become a Living Swarm as detailed below. Your flavor changes how the swarm behaves. As a part of using this ability or as a Flourish on subsequent turns, you can choose to immediately become a humanoid again at any point within your swarm area (as detailed in the Living Swarm block below.)

Living Swarm

Crawling Swarm : 4 m connected surfaces swarm
Movement : Walk 3 (+Crawling Swarm: Climb 3) (+Flying Swarm: Fly 3)
Senses : Blindsense 0 m.
Health : 0
Vitality : Redirect all to character's Vitality.
Saves, Resistances, Immunities, Languages : Use character's values.

Choose any number of characters touching the swarm. They must make a saving throw or gain the Fear condition.
