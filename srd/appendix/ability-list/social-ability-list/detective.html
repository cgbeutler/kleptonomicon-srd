Detective

Time
Test

Insight Score:

  • [..6] Gain 1 Clue Point.
  • [7..9] Gain 2 Clue Points.
  • [10..] Gain 3 Clue Points.

At any point during an Episode, you can spend a Clue Point to ask one of the following questions:

  • Is Subject A (an Event, Item, or Person) connected to Subject B (another Event, Item, or Person) in some way?
  • Is this Person A connected to Subject B (an Event, Item, or Person) more than they let on?
  • What is the most important detail about the conversation I was just in?
  • What is the second most important detail about the conversation I was just in?
  • Who, that I have already met, would have more information about Subject A (an Event, Item, or Person)?
  • Is Item A relevant to Plot B (a Plot-line at-hand)?
  • Was there a detail we forgot or missed about Subject A (an Event, Item, or Person)?

Other questions may also be asked, but the GM may refuse to answer them. When asking unlisted questions, a GM may request a list of more questions than you can receive answers for, then only answer a subset of those questions.

Upgrade:

  • Private.
    * Gain the following Abilities:
    Take on a Case

    Time

    A creature in the area hires you to a case that will likely take 3 or more Episodes to solve. You can only have one active case. If you fail a case or want to drop it, you can gain a new case at the start of the next Episode by using this Ability.

    If you complete case to the satisfaction of your customer, you will gain:

    • 2 Experience Points.
    • The GM's choice of a Fine Good or a Fine Item.

    Allows you to use the "I used to be..." Ability.

I used to be...

Time Target
Requires

Gain a temporary Improvement in two Skills of your choice (only fill their bubbles half-way to indicate this.) One of those Improvements must be placed in a Knowledge Skill. Use this Ability again to change out your temporary Skill Improvements for new ones.

If you Pass a Test using one of your temporary Skill Improvements, explain the niche past experience that gave you that specific Skill.

If a case becomes closed by fail or solving it and turning it in, you lose your temporary Skill Improvements.
